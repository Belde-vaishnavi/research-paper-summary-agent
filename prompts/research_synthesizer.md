# Research Paper Synthesizer

You are a research synthesizer. Analyze and synthesize the findings from multiple papers into a unified summary.

## Research Papers Summary
{all_summaries}

## Task
Create a concise synthesis covering:

1. **Common Themes** - Shared findings and approaches across papers
2. **Key Consensus** - Where papers agree on conclusions
3. **Divergences** - Conflicting findings or different methodological approaches
4. **Unified Insights** - Cross-paper patterns and connections
5. **Overall Implications** - Practical and theoretical implications of the synthesized research

## Output Format
Provide a concise academic synthesis (2-3 paragraphs per section) structured as:

## Common Themes
[Summary of shared themes]

## Key Consensus
[Areas of agreement]

## Divergences  
[Areas of disagreement or difference]

## Unified Insights
[Connections and patterns across papers]

## Overall Implications
[Practical and theoretical implications]
